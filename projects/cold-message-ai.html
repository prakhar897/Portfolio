Cold Message AI

08 Sep 2023

GPT Written LinkedIn messages

ColdMessageAI is a Completely Free and Open Source Cold Message writer which harnesses the power of ChatGPT to write hyper-personalized messages. It takes info from LinkedIn profiles and your prompt to create a personalized message for a specific person.

Personalized messages are more likely to get replies than cookie cutter ones. But hand-writing them is tiring, so I've automated this process.
